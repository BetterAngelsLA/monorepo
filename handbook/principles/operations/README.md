# Operations

> **Status: placeholder.** This folder is reserved for possible future
> **operations principles** — how the team itself works (roles,
> coordination, decision rights, rituals, request handling). It is
> **not** about product decision tradeoffs.

## Scope today

Operations principles are **out of scope for now.** The active
principles work is focused on a single type:

- [**Team principles**](../team/principles.md) — how we make
  product-decision tradeoffs.

We may add operations principles, engineering principles, or other
principle types in the future. For now we want to test one type
(team) before expanding.

## What lives here

- [`principles.md`](./principles.md) — a stub listing operations topics
  that surfaced during the team-principles work and may eventually
  become operations principles. Today it's a parking lot, not a
  rulebook.

## When to add to this folder

Add a topic here when it's clearly **about how the team operates**
rather than about a product tradeoff. Example signals:

- The question is "who owns this?" or "how do we coordinate?" rather
  than "should we build this?"
- The answer would apply regardless of which product is involved.
- It governs **inputs to product decisions** (e.g., how internal
  requests are intake'd) rather than the decisions themselves.

If the topic is really a product tradeoff, it belongs in
[`team/`](../team/) instead.
