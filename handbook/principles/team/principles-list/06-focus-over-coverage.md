# 6. Focus over Coverage

We prioritize solving a specific problem for a named persona over broad
solutions.
General solutions dilute adoption.

- _Decision check:_ Name the single persona this is for. If the answer is more than one, narrow it.
- _Status:_ enforceable.

## Notes

- **Persona vs. Customer** — this is the distinction that makes #6 and
  [#8 Scalable Solutions](./08-scalable-solutions-over-individual-customers-needs.md)
  consistent rather than contradictory:
  - **Persona = role** (Caseworker, Volunteer, Admin). #6 says narrow
    the role.
  - **Customer = organization** (a nonprofit org, a CoC). #8 says don't narrow
    the organization.
  - A feature should target **one Persona across many Customers** — not
    one Customer's specific Persona. See
    [terminology → Persona vs. Customer](../reference/terminology.md#persona-vs-customer).
