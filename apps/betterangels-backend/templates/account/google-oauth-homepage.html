Better Angels United Inc. is a nonprofit organization focused on ending the homelessness crisis in Los Angeles. The Better Angels platform is designed to empower stakeholders—unhoused clients, case managers, and service providers—by leveraging world-class technology to improve efficiency and effectiveness in addressing homelessness. The platform is hosted with managed services through AWS and is available on app stores for mobile access. Additionally, online portals are provided for administrative functions for internal Better Angels staff. What Better Angels Does with Google User Data

When a user connects with Google, we collect their OpenID, email, and profile information to create an account within the Better Angels system. No other Google data is collected. This information is used solely for account creation and authentication purposes.

How Better Angels Enhances User Functionality

Better Angels provides tools for those in the homeless ecosystem to track an unhoused individual's journey through various services such as housing, medical care, and workforce development. The platform offers shelters tools to book, list, and manage their shelters, and allows unhoused clients to self-service their shelter needs, all the way to permanent housing.

The app also enables caseworkers to:

  • Track cases in a way that allows them to easily share information and understand case history.
  • Track housing options and facilitate attribute matching so that caseworkers and homeless individuals can easily find suitable units.
  • Match people with available services and shelters.

Service providers benefit from reporting tools that ensure their data is up-to-date and accurately reflects the impact they are making for their clients and within their community. The app uses role-based access control (RBAC) and encryption to protect any entered user data.
